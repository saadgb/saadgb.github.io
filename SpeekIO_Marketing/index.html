SpeekIO make Recruitment easier and more affordable

Are you still manually fighting your way through every CV you receive as the first step in your recruitment process? Don’t you wish that there was a way that you could have saved more than half of your time spent screening unfit candidates? The solution is here, SpeekIO. With our simple and intuitive video pre-screening tool, you can now simply optimize and streamline your recruitment process while saving time and finding the optimal candidates. In other words: Forget everything about the unstructured, outdated review of every single resume – step into a whole new recruitment world. Welcome to SpeekIO.
